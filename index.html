About Us

La Tango Dance Club is the pioneer of tango dancing scene in east Malaysia. Started by Rachel Kumar in 2008 out of passion in the tango dancing. It has since fuel the growing community and successfully organize series of international tango workshop by bringing renown world class maestro all the way from Argentina.
